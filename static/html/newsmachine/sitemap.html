Reuters NewsMachine The Ultimate News Source

NewsMachine consists of four content groups and three functions.
The four content groups are News, Business, Sports and Alt.News.
The three functions are News.Search, My.News and News.Talk.
News: up-to-the-minute reports from Reuters bureaus around the world

Business: the stories shaping today's economic and business news

Sports: all the latest sports stories

Alt.News: offbeat news for a change of pace

News.Search: a powerful tool to help you find stories of special personal interest

My.News: a customized report of stories reflecting the topics you choose

News.Talk: an area to discuss what is in the news with people around the world
