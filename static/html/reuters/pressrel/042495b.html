S&P, REUTERS OFFER NEW INSURANCE INFORMATION PACKAGE

London, 24 April - Standard & Poor's (S&P) and Reuters today announced they have joined forces to provide a package of important insurance and financial data on-line to Reuters Insurance Briefing (RIB) subscribers.

The new offering has three main components:

  • S&P's Focus magazine which provides listings of the claims paying ability ratings of insurers and market analysis.

  • A live feed of S&P's Creditwire which will carry changes in S&P's claims paying ratings that occur between issues of Focus.

  • Selected S&P "ISI" rating changes.

Tony Evans, Director, Sales and Marketing (Europe) of Standard & Poor's Insurance Rating Services, said, "This exciting development gives a large sector of the insurance community on-line access to Standard & Poor's insurance data for the first time."

Peter Williams, Director of Reuters Insurance Information Services, said, "Insurance credit data from Standard & Poor's has been frequently requested by RIB customers. This important additional source of information will greatly enhance the usefulness of the service."

Reuters Insurance Briefing is a rapidly updating information service which combines global, breaking news on the insurance industry with a five-year archive of information drawn from some 800 business publications. These sources include the full text of many key insurance publications as well as reporting from Reuters journalists world-wide. Access is also provided to foreign exchange data, financial indices and share prices from the world's stock exchanges.

The easy-to-use Microsoft Windows interface, fast central host processor and global communications network make RIB accessible internationally to anyone using a PC and telephone line.

Standard & Poor's is the world's leading credit rating agency. Its specialist insurance rating division has over 60 insurance analysts based in its London, New York, Paris and Melbourne offices and offers global coverage of the insurance industry, rating and reporting on the financial strength of over 4,000 insurers and reinsurers world-wide.

Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ symbol: RTRSY) supplies the global business community and news media with a wide range of products including real-time financial data, transaction systems, access to numerical and textual historical databases, news, graphics, still photos and news video. Reuter Group companies design and install trading room systems. Information is obtained from around 220 exchanges and over-the-counter markets, from some 4,130 subscribers who contribute data directly to Reuters, and from a network of about 1,640 journalists, photographers and cameramen. Some 296,700 clients access Reuter information, some using their own terminals to do so.
