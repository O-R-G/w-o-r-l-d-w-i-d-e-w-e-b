DOCUPRO TO DELIVER REUTERS REAL-TIME FINANCIAL DATA TO F/X ALERTsm CUSTOMERS

New York, June 19,1995 - Reuters America Inc. (NASDAQ: RTRSY) today announced that it will begin to deliver real-time prices and financial news to DocuPro's F/X Alert sm customers. Reuters information will be broadcast nationwide by DocuPro to its wireless paging device 24 hours a day.

Reuters coverage of foreign exchange, international fixed income, derivatives and futures markets will now be available to DocuPro's extensive customer base throughout the U.S. for the first time. F/X Alert, which will be marketed and sold by both companies, will begin to deliver Reuters information by the end of July.

In the U.S., Reuters POCKETWATCH® financial pager service operates in the greater New York, Chicago and Boston areas. Current subscribers will be offered DocuPro's F/X Alert as a replacement, providing them with nationwide coverage and expanded information content.

In Canada, DocuPro's F/X Alert serves only the Toronto area. Their subscribers will be offered Reuters POCKETWATCH®, which provides expanded wireless geographic access throughout Canada.

Sarah Dunn, Senior Vice President, Area Marketing, Reuters America, said, "We are pleased to be working with DocuPro, a leader in the field of wireless information delivery. F/X Alert provides an effective way of distributing up-to-the-minute, expanded Reuters data to market professionals "on the go" throughout the U.S. We look forward to a mutually productive relationship."

Peter Kibler, President of DocuPro, Inc., commented on the relationship with Reuters, saying, "The high-quality information available from Reuters will further enhance DocuPro's service offerings to North American customers, whereby delivery of real-time information is essential to the mobility needs of professionals. We are highly impressed by the caliber and professionalism of Reuters and feel this relationship will provide a quality continuum to our clients. We welcome Reuters aboard and look forward to a long-lasting and mutually rewarding relationship."


DocuPro, Inc. is a leader in wireless information services, providing 24-hour coverage of real-time financial quotes and news. The company's advanced, pocket-sized financial monitoring service, F/X Alert, covers global markets 24 hours a day, over wireless networks to miniature computing devices and alpha pagers of fixed-income traders, as well as brokers, money managers and investors. This battery-powered pager keeps traders abreast of time-critical global foreign exchanges, provides current GovPX U.S. Treasuries prices, and international fixed income and derivatives market movements 24 hours a day. Foreign exchange coverage is carefully balanced with that of futures and cash commodities, money markets and international bonds and indices. News headlines may also be monitored, using the unit's 80-character, wide-screen display. F/X Alert is available throughout the United States, Canada, Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands.

Reuters America Inc. is the chief operating division in the Americas of Reuters Holdings PLC, the international news and financial information services group. Reuters supplies the global business community and news media with a wide range of products, including real-time financial data, transaction systems, access to numerical and textual historical databases, news, graphics, still photos and news video. Information is obtained from around 220 exchanges and over-the-counter markets, from approximately 4,130 subscribers who contribute data directly to Reuters, and from a network of about 1,640 journalists, photographers and cameramen. Some 300,000 clients access Reuter information, some using their own terminals to do so.
