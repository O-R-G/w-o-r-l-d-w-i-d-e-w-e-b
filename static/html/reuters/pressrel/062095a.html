REUTERS TO MEET INCREASED DEMAND FOR LOCAL BUSINESS NEWS WITH STATE-BY-STATE COVERAGE OF US COMPANIES

New York, June 26, 1995 -- Reuters America Inc. today announced that it will enhance its business news service for newspaper and broadcast clients with individual state-by-state reports covering more than 6,000 companies in the U.S. Reuters State Business News, developed to meet the increased demand for local and regional company news, will be launched in September as part of Reuters Business Report, a leading source of business news for broadcast, electronic and print media.

Reuters State Business News will provide news reports on companies in each of the 50 U.S. states. This will give media clients access to Reuters vast financial and industry reporting infrastructure now used by banks, brokerage firms and other professionals.

The announcement was made by Paul Eedle, Executive Vice President and Editor America, Reuters America Inc., at NEXPO '95, the annual technical exposition and conference of the Newspaper Association of America.

Reuters Business Report clients will now receive significantly more news throughout the day about companies and other market-moving developments that impact business in their specific states. Home state business news coverage will be provided to clients free of charge. Clients will also have the opportunity to select Reuters State Business News coverage for states other than their home states for a modest, incremental fee.

"Reuters State Business News will give our clients a tremendous reporting advantage by providing essential state news based on the same timely and insightful information used by financial industry professionals," said Mr Eedle. "It further strengthens the position of Reuters Business Report as a key source of news for local business pages and local broadcasts -- bringing a significantly heightened focus on state news to our already comprehensive national and international coverage.

Reuters Business Report provides fast, accurate and thorough business and financial coverage for business sections of daily newspapers and broadcasters. Drawing on the resources of 128 Reuter news bureaus around the world, including 27 bureaus in the U.S. and Canada, Reuters Business Report provides a daily package of business stories covering major global events and financial markets. It also includes a business outlook on the day's expected activity, authoritative market reports, corporate news briefs and regular weekly columns on specific topics ranging from computers to personal asset management.

Reuters America Inc. is the chief operating division in the Americas of Reuters Holdings PLC. Reuters supplies the news media and the global business community with a wide range of products, including news, graphics, still photos, news video, access to historical databases, real-time financial data and transaction systems. Information is obtained from a network of more than 1,600 journalists, photographers and cameramen, as well as 220 exchanges and over-the counter markets and more than 4,000 subscribers who contribute financial data directly to Reuters.
