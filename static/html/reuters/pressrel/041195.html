FIRST QUARTER REVENUE MATCHES EXPECTATIONS

New York, April 11, 1995 -- Preliminary indications of first quarter revenue matched the Board's expectations, Sir Christopher Hogg, Chairman of Reuters Holdings PLC, told the company’s annual general meeting today.

The company would be reporting in detail on revenue when it issued its quarterly trading statement on April 28, he added.

The meeting re-elected as directors, Sir Christopher Hogg, Pehr Gyllenhammar and Peter Job, the chief executive. Sir David Walker, who retired having been appointed by the Board since the last annual general meeting, was also re-elected.

Shareholders also approved amendments to the company’s articles of association at an extraordinary general meeting which followed the AGM. Among the changes approved was the ability in future for the directors to make arrangements for shareholders to view and hear the proceedings of any shareholder meeting through audio-visual links from satellite venues around the world.
