REUTERS ADDS HOOVER'S COMPANY PROFILES TO ITS DATABASE

NEW YORK, August 11, 1995--Reuters NewMedia Inc. has secured non-exclusive, worldwide rights from The Reference Press, Inc. to distribute Hoover's Company Profiles, a database of in-depth profiles of over 1,500 public and private US and international companies.

Hoover's Company Profiles include operations overviews, company strategies, histories, lists of products and key competitors, and up to 10 years of key financial and share price data on major established firms as well as on newer high-growth companies. Each company listing also features the names of senior executives, headquarters address, and phone and fax numbers.

Reuters will distribute this key company information as part of the Reuters Business Information database, which it markets to companies in a wide variety of industries. The company information will be available worldwide to users of the Reuters Business Briefing, Reuters Business Alert, and Reuters Insurance Briefing.

The agreement is the latest in a series of American content acquisition deals negotiated by Reuters NewMedia in recent months to strengthen the Reuters Business Information database. This database of about 2,000 international sources includes newspapers, magazines, trade and professional journals, databases, and news wires, including Reuters.

Key recent agreements with US-based providers include those with Phillips Business Information, Inc., Information Access Company (PROMT® and Computer Database®), CMP Publications, and Cahners Publishing Company.

The Reference Press, Inc. (Austin, TX) is a leading publisher and distributor of company information delivered to mass markets in both print and electronic media under the Hoover's Handbooks name. These include books and directories as well as on-line, facsimile, computer diskette, personal digital assistant, and CD-ROM formats.
