REUTERS BRIGHTSTAR TRANSMIT THE TRANS-ATLANTIC BROADCAST OF THE FRENCH OPEN TENNIS TOURNAMENT

New York, May 20 -- Reuters BrightStar Communications, a leading provider of international satellite broadcasting, will transmit USA Networks' broadcasts of the French Open Tennis Tournament. The tournament will be broadcast from Paris over two weeks, from May 29 to June 9.

"There are many options for getting a signal across the Atlantic," said Mark Haden, Field Technical Manager, USA Networks. "But, when comparing quality versus price, Reuters BrightStar is an easy decision. There is the potential for a great tennis match to go long. The BrightStar sales department was very flexible in working with us providing continuing service in that case. Reuters BrightStar is top-notch."

According to Paul Eedle, Editor and Senior Vice President, Reuters America Inc., "BrightStar has all the elements and expertise in place, in Europe and the United Sates, to support the broadcast of major international sporting events such as The French Open, one of five 'Grand Slam' tennis events held worldwide. This is the seventh consecutive year BrightStar will support this event."

Broadcasts will be transmitted aboard the Intelsat-K satellite, one of the most powerful satellites in the world, capable of providing high-powered connections as far north as Scandinavia, as far east as The Commonwealth of Independent State, south to Argentina, and as far west as Central North America. "Intelsat-K is unequaled in flexibility and versatility, and is the ideal vehicle to support broadcasts of a sports event the magnitude of The French Open," added Eedle.

Reuters BrightStar, one of the leading providers of international satellite transmissions services in the world, provides regular and special event transmissions and has built a wide range of satellite management services for its customers. BrightStar provides cost and quality-effective services via state-of-the-art international Broadcast Centers which are located in London, Moscow and Washington, D.C. USA Network is one of BrightStar's largest international television customers. Other customers include ABC, CBS, CNN, Fox, and the National Basketball Association.

Reuters America, Inc. is the chief operating division in the Americas of Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ:RTRSY), the news and financial services group.
