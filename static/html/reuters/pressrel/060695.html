DEALING 2000-2 BREAKS THROUGH 20,000 MATCHES

New York, June 6, 1995 -- Reuters Dealing 2000-2 automated trading system for foreign exchange has achieved its latest target of 20,000 trades, the company said.

The target was set on February 14 at the time of the company's 1994 results announcement, following the achievement of the previous target of 10,000 trades a day earlier this year.

The three-year old product was the first of its kind for the foreign exchange market when it was launched in April 1992. It took about one year to hit 1,000 trades a day and about one further year to reach 5,000.

More than 740 sites are connected to the service in 24 countries with a further 60 sites to be installed shortly. Dealers can trade electronically on the system in 23 currency pairs such as the US dollar/Deutschmark, US dollar/yen, Deutschmark/lira and US dollar/Swiss franc, in minimum amounts of one million units.

The service has just been introduced to the Middle East, starting with Dubai.
