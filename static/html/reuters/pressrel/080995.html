REUTERS ADDS KEY INDUSTRY TITLES TO ITS BUSINESS DATABASE

NEW YORK, August 9, 1995--Reuters NewMedia Inc. has secured the non-exclusive, international rights to distribute full-text references and abstracts from some 85 newsletters and industry journals published by Phillips Business Information, Inc.

The newsletters, journals and magazines governed by the agreement are among the most important specialized titles covering the communications, high technology, banking and energy industries. Reuters will distribute this key industry content as part of the Reuters Business Information database which it markets to companies in a wide variety of industries.

The agreement covers communications and high technology titles including Data Channels, Electronic Messaging News, European Telecommunications, Internet Week, Multimedia Week, Mobile Phone News, Tactical Technology, Wireless Business & Finance, Advanced Intelligent Network News, Communications Today, European Media Business & Finance, Fiber Optics News, HDTV Report, Interactive Video News, ISDN News, Pacific Rim Telecommunications, Satellite News, Video Technology Week, and Washington Telecom News.

Banking journals include Bank Automation News, Financial Services Report, Treasury Manager’s Report, and Credit Risk Management Report.

Energy titles include 21st Century Fuels, Oil & Gas Interest Newsletter, Oxy-Fuel News, Gas Transactions Report, Octane Week, and Petroleum Finance Week.

The agreement also covers key industry marketing and public relations journals such as Healthcare PR & Marketing News, Interactive Marketing News, PR News, and min: Media Industry Newsletter.

According to Janet Pistone, Executive Vice President, Reuters NewMedia Inc.: “We are pleased to be able to augment the Reuters Business Information database with these fine Phillips Business Information titles in the high technology, banking and energy market segments that are important to our customers.

The new content will be available worldwide to users of the Reuters Business Briefing, Reuters Business Alert, and Reuters Insurance Briefing.

The agreement is the latest in a series of American content acquisition deals negotiated by Reuters NewMedia in recent months to strengthen the Reuters Business Information database. This database of about 2,000 international sources includes newspapers, magazines, trade and professional journals, databases, and news wires including Reuters.

Key recent agreements with US-based providers include those with Information Access Company, CMP Publications, Inc., and Cahners Publishing Company.

Phillips Business Information, Inc. is a division of Phillips Publishing International, Inc. (Potomac, MD) one of the largest newsletter and periodical publishers in the United States. Founded in 1974, Phillips Publishing International publishes more than 100 newsletters, magazines, directories, weekly newspapers, books, special reports and on-line services. Phillips provides information to business and consumer markets to support informed decisions about investments, health, business and public policy issues.

Reuters developed its Business Information database and on-line retrieval services to enable corporations and financial services companies to gather business intelligence from the best available sources. These on-line services provide ready access to a database containing current business news and business information on thousands of publicly-traded and unlisted companies dating back five years.

Reuters Business Information is marketed in the Americas by Reuters NewMedia Inc., a division of Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: RTRSY).
