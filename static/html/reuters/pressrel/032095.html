REUTERS ADDS CMP CONTENT TO BUSINESS INFORMATION DATABASE

NEW YORK, March 20, 1995--Reuters NewMedia Inc. has secured the international rights to distribute content from CMP Publications, Inc., a noted publisher of computing,high technology, and multimedia titles.

Reuters will distribute the CMP content as part of the Reuters Business Information product database which it markets to corporations.

The CMP list includes CommunicationsWeek, Communications Week International, InformationWeek, WINDOWS Magazine, Network Computing, HOME PC, Interactive Age, NetGuide, VARBusiness, Computer Reseller News, Computer Retail Week, OEM Magazine, Electronic Engineering Times, and Electronic Buyers’ News.

CMP content will be available to users of the Reuters Business Briefing, Reuters Insurance Briefing and Reuters Business Alert services.

Reuters developed its Business Information products to enable corporations to gather business intelligence from a vast array of sources. These products provide ready access to a database containing business information on thousands of publicly-traded and unlisted companies dating back five years. There are more than 700 international sources including newspapers, magazines, trade and professional journals, databases and news wires, including Reuters.

Recent content acquisition agreements with providers such as CMP Publications will expand the list of international sources to more than 2,000 as new titles become available on the Reuters database in the coming months.

In the U.S., Reuters America markets these products to the financial services industry and the financial functions within corporations.

Reuters NewMedia markets to other functions within corporations, such as sales, marketing, corporate communications, legal, strategic planning, and management.

Reuters NewMedia Inc. is a U.S.-based company formed by Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: RTRSY) in 1994 to develop multi-media information and transaction services for emerging corporate and consumer markets.

Reuters America Inc. is the chief operating entity of Reuters Holdings in the Americas.

CMP Publications, Inc. (Manhasset, NY) is a leading provider of high technology publishing, marketing and information services reaching the full spectrum of information technology markets. CMP’s high technology publications reach the builders, the sellers and the users of computer and information technology.
