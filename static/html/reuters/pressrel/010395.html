REUTERS TO BECOME SOLE DISTRIBUTOR OF ITS SERVICES IN AUSTRALIA

Sydney, January 3, 1995-- Reuters is going to distribute its financial information and transaction products in Australia independently from 1996. Following a review by Reuters and AAP Information Services Pty Ltd (AAP), the exclusive distribution rights currently held by AAP will cease on December 31, 1995.

The products include domestic and international quotations and news covering foreign exchange and money markets, equities, commodities and financial futures. Reuters also provides transaction products enabling its customers to trade on their screens.

David Brocklehurst, Managing Director of Reuters Australia Pty Limited, said: "Australia is a major market in Asia/Pacific for Reuters. In distributing independently from 1996, as our international competitors already do, we shall be investing in a strong infrastructure. Clients will be informed of our new support arrangements as soon as they are finalized, and we shall be looking to assure them of our continued strong commitment to the Australian market."

Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: RTRSY) distributes all its products directly in about 120 countries. It maintains 190 international offices, including Sydney and Melbourne, with 1,470 editorial staff. Its products are available in all Australian cities.
