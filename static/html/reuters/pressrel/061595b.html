REUTERS DELIVERS PLATT'S ENERGY, PETROCHEMICAL AND METALS INFORMATION.

New York, June 19, 1995 - Reuters today announced an agreement with The McGraw-Hill Companies covering the distribution of Platt's Energy, Petrochemical and Metals information on the Reuter network.

This marks the first time key market data from both Reuters and Platt's can be received together on a single terminal or datafeed, worldwide. Previously, the Platt's services have only been available over the Standard & Poor's ComStock network.

Platt's Global Alert, Petrochemical Alert, Natural Gas Alert and Metals Alert are authoritative sources of news, market analysis and price assessments for the Energy, Petrochemical and non-Ferrous Metals industries respectively.

The services are delivered in real time directly to the Reuter Terminal (RT) or via the SelectFeed Plus datafeed to the customer's own network.

Platt's Global Alert and Reuter Energy 2000 are complementary services which combine Reuters leading market information, news and applications products with Platt's widely used cash market assessments, news and commentary.

A division of Standard & Poor's, Platt's is a leading supplier of print and real-time market information to the energy, petrochemical and metals industries. Standard & Poor's is a division of The McGraw-Hill Companies, Inc. (NYSE symbol :MHP).

Reuters (NASDAQ:RTRSY) supplies the global business community and news media with a wide range of products including real-time financial data, transaction services, access to numerical and textual historical databases, news, graphics, still photos and news video. Information is obtained from around 220 exchanges and over-the-counter markets, from approximately 4,130 subscribers who contribute data directly to Reuters, and from a network of about 1,640 journalists, photographers and cameramen. Some 300,000 clients access Reuter information, some using their own terminals to do so.
