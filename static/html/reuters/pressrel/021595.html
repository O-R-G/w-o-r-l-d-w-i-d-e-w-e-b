REUTERS ADDS CAHNERS CONTENT TO BUSINESS INFORMATION DATABASE


NEW YORK, NY, February 15, 1995--Reuters NewMedia Inc. has secured the international, non-exclusive rights to distribute content from Cahners Publishing Company, which is the largest publisher of specialized business and consumer magazines in the U.S.

Reuters will distribute a subset of the 90-publication Cahners list as part of the Reuter Business Information products database which it markets to corporations. That subset is likely to include between 20 and 40 titles in the first year.

The Cahners list includes some of the most notable publications in the high technology, consumer, manufacturing, design, entertainment, publishing, medical and pharmaceutical industries. These include Datamation, EDN, Electronic Business Buyer, Government Computer News, Fibre Optic Product News, Biomedical Products, Pharmaceutical Processing, Design News, Industrial Distribution, Packaging Digest, Prepared Foods, Publishers Weekly and Broadcasting & Cable. Reuters had previously secured the rights to distribute content from the Cahners publications Variety and Daily Variety.

According to Alexa Smith, Business Information Products Manager, Reuters NewMedia: "Under this agreement Reuters can choose from nearly half of the 90 titles on the Cahners list for inclusion in the Reuter Business Information products database. "The content will be available to users of the Reuter Business Briefing, Reuter Insurance Briefing and Reuter Business Alert services. This will represent, in several instances, the first electronic online versions of these Cahners titles."

Reuters developed its Business Information products to enable corporations to gather business intelligence from a vast array of sources. These products provide ready access to a database containing business information on thousands of publicly-traded and unlisted companies dating back five years. There are more than 600 international sources including newspapers, magazines, trade and professional journals, databases and news wires, including Reuters.

Later this year, as Cahners titles plus databases recently acquired from Thomson Corporation division Information Access Company (IAC) become available to Reuter customers, the list of international sources will have surpassed 1,800.

In the U.S., Reuters America markets these products to the financial services industry and the financial functions within corporations. Reuters NewMedia markets to all other functions within corporations.

Reuters NewMedia Inc. is a U.S.-based company formed by Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: RTRSY) in 1994 to develop multi-media information and transaction services for emerging corporate and consumer markets.

Reuters America Inc. is the chief operating entity of Reuters Holdings in the Americas.

Cahners Publishing Company is a unit of Reed Elsevier plc, one of the world's largest publishing and information entities.
