REUTERS AND J. J. KENNY LAUNCH NEW SERVICE FOR THE MUNICIPAL MARKETPLACE

New York, May 16, 1995 -- Reuters and J. J. Kenny Co., Inc. today announced the introduction of MuniMarket, a new service for professionals in the municipal securities marketplace. MuniMarket is the first in a series of municipal securities information products being developed jointly by both companies.

The service, which will be available to Reuters customers in the U.S., provides comprehensive municipal market coverage in easily accessible form. It can be delivered directly to the user's Reuter Terminal (RT) or, via datafeed, to the customer's own network.

Among the key services being provided by J.J. Kenny under this agreement are: the Blue List Bond Ticker with real-time, record-based offerings and price changes, the Blue List Broker Wire with page-based bid-wanteds from the top 15 brokers, offerings and dollar bond quotes and the S & P Municipal One Million Index. Also included will be Kenny Drake Market Data, which provides up to 600 screens of real-time, record based active dollar bonds, bid wanteds and offerings, and the MSRB Transaction Report providing price transparency information on inter-dealer transactions in municipal securities.

A dedicated team of expert journalists from Reuters provides detailed coverage and commentary on news affecting the national and regional municipal markets. Reuters coverage includes ratings changes, new issues, municipal buy-side interviews, political news and regional economic data. MuniMarket also includes Reuters Treasury and money market news plus live data contributed by leading brokers.

Debra Heffernan, Director of Marketing, Municipal Services, Reuters America Inc., said: "We are delighted to be working with J. J. Kenny on the development of joint products. Kenny's comprehensive municipal information and evaluation services will provide our customers with a high-quality, cost-effective solution to their municipal information needs."

Donald H. Totter, Senior Vice President and General Manager, J.J. Kenny Information and Evaluation Services, said: "At J.J. Kenny we are pleased to release the first of many joint products with Reuters for the municipal marketplace. We have been extremely impressed with the commitment Reuters has shown to our market and our customers' needs. Combining the depth of Kenny's databases with Reuters technically sophisticated open architecture platforms brings the most comprehensive solutions to municipal professionals."

A wholly owned subsidiary of The McGraw-Hill Companies, Inc. (NYSE:MHP), J. J. Kenny Co., Inc. is the largest municipal bond brokers broker and the leading supplier of municipal securities information and evaluation services.

Reuters America is the chief operating division in the Americas of Reuters Holdings PLC (NASDAQ: RTRSY). Reuters supplies the global business community and news media with a wide range of products, including real-time financial data, transaction systems, access to numerical and textual historical databases, news, graphics, still photos and news video. Information is obtained from around 220 exchanges and over-the-counter markets, from approximately 4,130 subscribers who contribute data directly to Reuters, and from a network of about 1,640 journalists, photographers and cameramen. Some 300,000 clients access Reuter information, some using their own terminals to do so.
