REUTERS NAMES DOUG ALEXANDER PRESIDENT AND CEO OF REALITY ONLINE

New York, April 20, 1995 - Reuters (NASDAQ: RTRSY) announced today the appointment of Doug Alexander to President and Chief Executive Officer of Reality Online Inc., a Reuter company which develops investment software for individuals.

Alexander, who co-founded the company in 1986 along with Mark Goldstein, previously served as Editor-in-Chief and Executive Vice President, responsible for the design and content of Reality's products and services.

Robert Russel, Senior Vice President, Equities, Reuters America, said, "Doug Alexander is the right person to take over at the helm of Reality Online at this stage of its development. His background and experience will enable the company to continue to develop innovative services for the individual investor as well as the financial services professional."

Alexander succeeds Reality Online co-founder Mark Goldstein, who, after guiding the company through its integration into the Reuter organization, has resigned to pursue other interests. Goldstein will act as a consultant to Reality on certain key projects.

Douglas Alexander graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a B.S. degree in electrical engineering and received a B.S. in economics from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

Reality Online Inc., founded in 1986, develops and operates Reuters Money Network, one of the largest online services dedicated to personal investing combining powerful investment software with a full array of online financial information, news and market data. Reality also publishes Wealthbuilder, an investment-oriented financial planning software program for individuals.

Reuters is the international news and financial information services group.
